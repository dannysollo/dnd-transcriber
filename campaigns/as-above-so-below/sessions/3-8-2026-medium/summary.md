# Session Summary — 3/8/2026

## Pre-Departure: Aziah Shopping Montage
The session opened with the party's legendary item reveals and final farewells in Aziah. Each character had a significant upgrade: **Kali** debuted *Death's Eternal March*, a +1 breastplate forged from the Greater Angel's own chitin and sinew, granting adaptive damage resistance, bloodied-state bonuses, and one legendary resistance per day — plus supporting accessories (Brooch of Shielding, Guardian Emblem, Periapt of Wound Closure). **Aella** unveiled her redesigned Eldritch Cannon, dubbed "Angel of Death" — a miniature angel construct weaponizing the crystallized Eye of Azrael to recreate its fear aura before self-detonating. **Vixeena** equipped a legendary sorcerer's focus that boosts spell DCs and attack rolls, allows cursing targets on metamagic use, and can restore sorcery points at the cost of true damage if she breaks concentration. **Belle** drank the Greater Angel's blood under Sinclair's recommendation, which slowly upgraded its processing speed and memory base — enabling it to begin forming introspective thoughts (a Disco Elysium–inspired thought cabinet mechanic). Danny explained the thematic throughline: each item involves lowering Normality in some way, whether through the Greater Angel's lingering essence, a weaponized gaze, reality distortion via Glimmer, or mysterious biological integration. Kali visited Din one final time before departure, warning her to remain vigilant about Binah and receiving a warm but understated farewell. The party departed Aziah by train for Fensworth.

## Arrival in Fensworth
The journey gave the party time to debrief about Fensworth. Aella laid out ground rules — most critically, that no one is to reveal she was the one who removed her brother Nico's foot to prevent him from being conscripted. The party, with varying levels of confidence-inspiring responses, agreed. On arrival, Nico (full name Reneiko) met them at the station — immediately embracing Aella, showing visible relief. During the walk into town he gave the party a tour: a general store, foreman's office, blacksmith (Vulcan, Aella's mentor), assay office, chapel, a cavern on Beacon Street, mines to the east, pastures and a dam to the west. Fensworth reads as a blue-collar Appalachian mining town; the party's armor and weapons made them obvious outsiders.

## The Nico Problem
The most significant revelation of the session: approximately two months ago, Nico began having recurring dreams of a blurry woman whose face he cannot recall, waking each time with new magic spells and a sense of peace and safety. His current repertoire includes Prestidigitation and Fireball. When Vixeena Glimmer-scanned him, she registered **48 Glimmer** — extraordinary for someone who has never been near labs or Sephirot. Kali's insight check (28) confirmed Nico was mostly forthcoming but slightly evasive, and she heard a quiet ticking sound when he cast — similar to but less extreme than the Tehom-adjacent ticking she's encountered before. The group privately theorized about the dream woman's identity but reached no conclusions. Aella gave her parents 100 gold, and the family dinner was tense-warm, with Aella's father asking after her hair and career, and her mother quietly noting she seems to have found a man in the city (outed by Belle).

After the family retired, Aella privately examined Nico's prosthetic leg. The **perpetually spinning clockwork disc** that served as its power source is gone — but the leg functions perfectly when worn, depowering only when removed. Using her compass, Aella tracked the missing component back to the house, almost certainly to Nico himself. She also searched the attic workshop and found a hidden compartment behind a false drawer-back containing: earlier prototype blueprints, and an **undelivered sealed letter from Alexander Nouveau** to an unnamed colleague. The letter describes a project he called **"the vessel"** — something he oversaw but wasn't directly involved in — and expresses deep moral conflict about whether it should continue or its results destroyed. Crucially, he writes the project is *"implemented but currently inaccessible"* and should remain so indefinitely unless someone actively changes that. The letter was never delivered.

## Investigation and Mine Arc
The next morning the party reconvened, shared everything, and began running down leads. Judah, reached via conduit, had no knowledge of anyone gaining powers through dreams of women. Aella's parents knew of no surviving colleagues of Alexander's in Fensworth. Vulcan greeted Aella warmly, critiqued her gun's unnecessary barrel thickness (since the propellant is magical, not chemical), and offered to vent it — which will reduce her prone movement penalty from 15 to 10 feet once complete. The foreman, initially evasive and dismissive, capitulated under Kali's deception bluff (28) and admitted the truth: three miners were trapped in Branch 7, returned unharmed but completely silent, then skipped town the next morning. The miners won't go near it again.

The party purchased canaries from a hostile general store owner (8 gold for two, after attempted negotiation made it worse), entered Branch 7, and discovered **Tehom seeping through the water table** throughout the mine. Normality was normal — this wasn't a ritual weakening, just the groundwater itself being contaminated with Tehom. Investigation confirmed a freak cave-in had cracked a section of ceiling, exposing the miners to a sudden Tehom emergence — not intentional, not sinister in origin, just the world bleeding. The party set up a **Barotraumatic Sublimation ritual** powered by Vixeena's CSF and linked to the Mer-power ritual to make it self-sustaining, clearing the Tehom from the mineshaft.

## Cliffhanger
Racing the incoming storm, the party sheltered at Aella's family home. As the storm raged — including an ominous cracking sound, as if the earth splitting — the family boarded windows and waited. As the storm died down and Nico went to get tools from the basement, **he stepped directly into inky black Tehom that had flooded up from below**. End of session.
